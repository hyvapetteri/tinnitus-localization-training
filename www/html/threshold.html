Threshold test

Now we'll find your hearing threshold for a test sound.

Click play when you're ready to start. There will be a constant hissing sound that starts from very quiet (you won't hear it at first, just wait a little), and gets louder. When you hear the sound, press the 'I hear the sound'-button and keep it pressed. Keep the button pressed until you can't hear the sound anymore and release the button.
Keep repeating this (hear the sound → keep pressing button; can't hear the sound → release button) until the meter below the button fills up.
